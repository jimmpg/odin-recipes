Es Teh Manis

Description

A classic, refreshing beverage made from freshly brewed black tea, sweetened with sugar, and served chilled over a generous amount of ice cubes. It is the perfect thirst-quencher for a hot day..

Ingredients

  • 2 tea bags of jasmine black tea
  • 200 ml of hot water
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A glass full of ice cubes

Steps

  1. Brew the tea
  2. Add the sugar
  3. Cool it down
  4. Add the ice cubes
  5. Serve
  • Ayam Goreng
  • Nasi Goreng
