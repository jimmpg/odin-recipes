Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is a classic Indonesian fried rice dish known for its distinct smoky, savory, and subtly sweet flavor profile.

Unlike regular fried rice, the defining characteristic of Nasi Goreng is the use of kecap manis (a thick, sweet soy sauce) which caramelizes the rice, giving it a deep golden-brown color and a rich taste.

Ingredients

  1. White Rice
  2. Margarine
  3. Soy Sauce
  4. Salt

Steps

  • Heat the margarine in a skillet
  • Add the rice to the skillet
  • Add soy sauce and salt
  • Stir until well combined
  • Serve
