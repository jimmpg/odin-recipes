Ayam Goreng

Description

Crispy, golden-brown fried chicken marinated in a rich blend of traditional aromatic herbs and spices, including coriander, garlic, and turmeric. Deep-fried to perfection, it is juicy on the inside and packed with savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 8 shallots
  • 2 tsp of coriander seeds
  • 1 inch of turmeric
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, bruised
  • 3 daun salam
  • Cooking oil for deep-frying

Steps

  1. Blend the spices
  2. Braise the chicken
  3. Drain the chicken
  4. Deep-fry until golden
  5. Serve
  • Ayam Goreng
  • Nasi Goreng
